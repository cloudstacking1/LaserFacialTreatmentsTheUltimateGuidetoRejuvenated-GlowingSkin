Understanding IPL Treatment: The Science Behind the Glow

IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) therapy works by delivering precise bursts of light energy into the deeper layers of your skin. This energy targets melanin and hemoglobin, breaking down pigmentation and tiny broken blood vessels.

At Selfie House NYC, our certified technicians adjust the wavelengths of light to suit your specific skin concerns. Whether you're struggling with sun spots, acne scars, or dullness, our IPL treatment can help reveal clearer, brighter skin.
